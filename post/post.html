ABOUT ITEM

Air Max Month is upon us, which means classic Air Max runners will be front and center for four-plus weeks to close out the Spring 2023 season. While the Air Max 1 may have captured the bulk of our attention, the Air Max 97 has been worthy of the spotlight as well thanks to some creative concepts that have surfaced. This upcoming release brings about a sunny yellow mix, appearing in a tie-dye like pattern inked onto the canvas underlay. A soft yellow makes an appearances on the tracks that circumvent the upper design, while the interior lining also appears in the blotchy mix of buttery yellow shades. A smooth white leather acts as the primary mudguard material, blending into the all-white midsole. Even the visible Air unit opts for a classic arctic white shade. See the official images ahead and stay tuned for a release on Nike.com. In other AM97 news, the Air Max 97 “Gold Bullet” is set to release in the US in March.
